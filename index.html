the nile

Nile River the longest river in the world, called the fathr of African rivers. It rises south of the Equator and flows northward through northeastern Africa to drain into the Mediterranean Sea. The Nile rises from lake victoria, the second largest fresh water body lake in the world, which has an area of more than 26,800 square miles the source of the nile is at jinja uganda.come visit the source of the nile. The river has tributaries such as the white nile, blue nile and the Victoria Nile, descending into the East African Rift valley over Murchison (Kabalega) Falls before entering the northern end of Lake Albert. Unlike Lake Victoria, Lake Albert is a deep, narrow lake with mountainous sides.
There the waters of the Victoria Nile unite with the lake waters, passing northward as the Albert Nile—a portion of the river. so you will not be disappointed visiting the nile river.
